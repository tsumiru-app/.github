# Code of Conduct

Tsumiru adopts the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/) (version 2.1) as its Code of Conduct.

In short: be respectful, welcoming, and constructive. We want participation in the Tsumiru community to be a positive experience for everyone, regardless of background or identity. Behavior that makes the community hostile or unwelcoming isn't acceptable.

The full text — including the standards we expect, scope, and enforcement guidelines — is available here:
**https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/**

## Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, please report it to the maintainers by opening an issue on the relevant repository, or by contacting a maintainer directly through GitHub. Reports will be handled promptly and in confidence.
